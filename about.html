Who is Tanishka?

Tanishka was born in Chennai, India and moved to Bellevue, Washington at the age of 8. Growing up, she's always had a curious mind, always on the search for new skills to learn. This might be why she has had experience in all kinds of things; from Kung Fu to Indian Classical Dance, or embroidery to oil pastel work, she loves exploring new hobbies and shooting big.

She currently attends school as a rising junior and is also a Running Start student at Bellevue College. Outside of school, she likes to run long-distance and watch the latest asian dramas on Netflix.
